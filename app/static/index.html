🔔 Setup Guide — Multi-Account
1. Enable Cost Explorer in your management account
2. Set AWS_ACCESS_KEY_ID / AWS_SECRET_ACCESS_KEY to management account creds
3. Or set AWS_MANAGEMENT_ACCOUNT_ROLE to an IAM Role ARN for cross-account access
4. Ensure the IAM policy includes ce:GetCostAndUsage + organizations:ListAccounts
